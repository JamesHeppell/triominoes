How to Play

  • Each tile has three corners numbered 0–5
  • Fill every board slot with a tile
  • Where two tiles share an edge, both corner values must match
  • Drag a tile onto the board · Tap to rotate it
  • Tinted tiles have extra constraints — shown by a badge:
  • 9 corners must sum to that number (one or two tiles)
  • ≠ all three corners must be different
  • ≡ all three corners must be equal
