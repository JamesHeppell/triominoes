Rules

The Tile

Each tile is an equilateral triangle with a number (0–5) at each corner. Drag tiles onto the board and tap to rotate.

The Adjacency Rule

Wherever two tiles share an edge, the values at both shared corners must match — like dominoes but with two numbers per edge.

Rotations

Tap any tile to cycle through 6 orientations. Three point up (▲) and three point down (▽) — the corner values shift with each tap.

Extra Constraints

Tinted tiles carry an extra constraint shown by a coloured badge at the centre. The badge turns green when satisfied and red when violated.

  • The corners of this tile must sum to the badge number. A badge between two tiles means both tiles' corners together must reach the target.

  • All three corner values must be different from each other.

  • All three corner values must be equal.
